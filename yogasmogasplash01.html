ABOUT
Yoga Smoga Inc., incorporated as of January 1, 2010, is a designer and retailer of Yoga inspired athletic apparel and accessories. The company was founded by Mr. Rishi Bali and headquartered in New York City.

The company is currently operating in a pre-launch stage with full retail operations to launch in 4Q of 2010. The company's performance apparel will be marketed under the Yoga Smoga brand name.
